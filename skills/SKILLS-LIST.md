# Your Installed Skills

These are the skills your AI assistant has available. Each one is a specialised set of abilities.

| Skill | What It Does | Example Prompt |
|---|---|---|
| `humanizer` | Makes AI writing sound like a real human wrote it | "Humanize this email" |
| `deep-research` | Researches any topic in depth using multiple sources | "Research the top 3 competitors in my industry" |
| `copywriting` | Writes persuasive marketing copy for websites, ads, landing pages | "Write my homepage headline and sub-headline" |
| `email-sequence` | Builds automated email sequences (welcome series, follow-ups, nurture) | "Build a 5-email welcome sequence for new leads" |
| `social-content` | Creates social media posts for LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram | "Write 7 LinkedIn posts for this week" |
| `content-creator` | Creates long-form content with SEO (blog posts, guides, articles) | "Write a 1000-word blog post about [topic]" |
| `sales-automator` | Drafts cold emails, follow-ups, proposals, and sales scripts | "Write a cold outreach email to a restaurant owner" |
| `brainstorming` | Structured idea generation — never gets stuck | "Give me 15 content ideas for my business" |
| `youtube-summarizer` | Summarises any YouTube video | "Summarise this YouTube video: [paste URL]" |
| `reddit-insights` | Mines Reddit for what your customers are actually saying | "What do people complain about in my industry on Reddit?" |
| `competitor-alternatives` | Analyses competitors and finds positioning opportunities | "Analyse my top 3 competitors" |
| `systematic-debugging` | Diagnoses and fixes problems step by step | "Something broke — here's the error: [paste error]" |
| `writing-plans` | Plans before executing complex tasks | "Plan how I could automate my client onboarding" |
| `avoid-ai-writing` | Removes AI writing patterns from any content | "Remove the AI-isms from this blog post" |
| `research-analyst` | Deep landscape and competitive research with structured reports | "Give me a full market research report on [industry]" |
| `prompt-engineer` | Improves AI prompts for better results | "Help me write a better prompt for [task]" |
